Colourful Life

Few months ago I started to write this journal. I was unsure about everything. Anyway I did it. And I wish I had a secret recipe to happiness to share with you all. But I think you have to find your own recipe. I know life can be hard, there will be days when you won’t want to get out of bed. But there will also be days when you feel so alive that you feel unbreakable. Like life can throw anything at you and you will just let it bounce off. You have to actively choose happiness and strength. And every now and then take a day off and just feel what you need to. But then fight back and get out of bed. So here I am, telling you guys that life is beautiful and messy and colorful but also a little grey at times. It’s goosebumps and mistakes. It’s love and laughters but it’s also disappointments at times. It’s a ride. You are you and never forget that or try to be someone else. Embrace yourself.
